Phylogeny

Phylogenetic analysis on molecular data is the use of molecules such as DNA or proteins to gain information on the evolutionary relationships between organisms or molecules. The result of the analysis is usually represented as a phylogenetic tree or dendrogram. The relatedness of the taxa (enteties) under investigation is computed directly from molecular sequences applying algorithms suitable for the given evolutionary distances and rates observed in the case studied. Estimation of evolutionary rates, codon usage bias, secondary structure constraints and other confounding factors are key to success.

Amber BioSciences have expertise in phylogenetic analysis ranging from whole organism to peptides. We can assist you with all steps from molecular alignment to phylogenetic trees.
