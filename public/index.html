The Amberbio app for mobile analysis and visualization of biological data sets

Available on the app store for iPhones and iPads

The app requires ios 8 or later.

About

The Amberbio app allows scientists to analyze and visualize biological data sets on mobile devices.

Many types of data can be analyzed in the app including protein, microRNA, and gene expression data.

Price

The app is currently a paid app. It will very soon become a free app with in-app purchases.

Details follow when the next version of the app is released.

Privacy

The Amberbio app respects user privacy.

No data is sent to the server.

All analysis is performed locally on the device.

There are no user names and log in.
