#proteins which are not
#beta in a list of BLAST
#hits (example in MySQL):


SELECT A.evalue, A.score,
A.identity, B.description
FROM Blast.Results AS A,
Uniprot.entries AS B
WHERE A.evalue<1e-10 &&
A.accnr=B.accnr && MATCH
(B.description) AGAINST
('+Actin -Beta' IN BOOLEAN
MODE);

Customized databases

High-throughput techniques deliver increasing amounts of biological data. The only way to provide structure and make data easily accessible is to store data in databases based on an efficient model like a relational database. Equally important is to use a database management system that is fast and reliable. Such databases may for example contain sequence and annotation information or microarray data.

We suggest that you have your database system on our servers and access your data through a web interface. The advantage of a web-interface is that all users are familiar with the web which reduces learning time and that data can be accessed from any place. Other advantages for you as a customer is the close to 100% accessibility, a reliable backup-facility and synergy effects of regular updates of databases shared by other customers.

Amber BioSciences is devoted to the so called LAMP-concept that covers all stages of web-development from operating and web-server systems to database management and programming. Software used under this concept is open source and free of charge which dramatically reduce customer investments.
