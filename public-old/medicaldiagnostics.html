Medical diagnostics

Proper disease diagnosis and prognosis is crucial for the choice of treatment. In recent years, it has become possible to measure thousands of variables, such as protein abundances, protein modifications, RNA abundances, DNA mutations, and metabolite levels, for each patient. These variables usually contain lots of diagnostic information but, due to the large amount of information, it is not trivial to actually map the variables to a diagnosis. Machine learning, which is a field of statistics, deals with exactly this problem. Various types of machine learning techniques, such as support vector machines, have been employed successfully to diagnose diseases.

Amber BioSciences offers to assist medical researchers during the various bioinformatical phases of the diagnosis problem, such as machine learning, experimental design, and a cost-benefit analysis of the whole process from the initial experiments to the final clinical product.
