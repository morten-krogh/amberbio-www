Amber BioSciences AB, a privately held company, was founded in April 2008 by three Swedish and Danish bioinformatics researchers, two of whom are still with the company.

The founders

Morten Krogh - Chief Executive Officer
Morten has a background in theoretical physics with a Ph.D. degree from Princeton University and postdoc experience from the Institute for Theoretical Physics, Santa Barbara, and CERN, Geneva. He then switched to the field of bioinformatics, first working as a postdoc in the Computational Biology and Biological Physics group at Lund University, later as an associate professor at the Create Health Centre in Lund. Morten has extensive experience collaborating with biological and medical research groups.
Ann-Sofie Albrekt
Ann-Sofie has a Ph.D. degree in molecular biology from Lund University, specializing in sequence analysis and molecular phylogenetic reconstruction. She has experience from the biotech industry both from working as a researcher and from working as a product specialist. Her recent endeavors were in the field of microarray analysis; she worked as a bioinformatician for the microarray facility SCIBLU Genomics at Lund University, where she assisted numerous customers with data analysis, experimental design and choice of software.

Bioinformaticians

Peter Olsson
Peter has a Ph.D. degree in applied biochemistry from Lund University, specializing in microarray analysis of abiotic stress responses. He has been responsible for an introductory course in bioinformatics at the Lund faculty of engineering's Master of Science in biotechnology for 5 years. He has also been working at the Swedish agricultural university with research into industrial crops, and recently with nutrigenomic at Lund University.
Elias Moberger
Elias has a master's degree in molecular biology with a specialization in bioinformatics. He has extensive programming skills and has had his own web programming company since 2004. Prior to working for Amber Biosciences, he has worked for a marketing research company. At Amber Biosciences, he has developed software for an editor, and for customer and employee surveys. Elias knows several computer languages and is especially fluent in Javascript and PHP. Of the statistics packages, he has extensive experience in Spss and R, and some knowledge of Matlab.
