Pathways

Analyzing complex biological and biochemical systems on a systems biology level is key to success in understanding the biology behind your data. Metabolic and signal transduction pathways constitute important building blocks in our understanding of cellular activity. Pathway analysis is often the end point of microarray or proteomics data analysis and the point at which the relationship between genes, proteins, drugs and diseases is described in words and publication-quality pictures.

We bridge the gap between gene lists and biological function by providing you with customised pathway analyses using algorithms, some of which are developed by ourselves. Our pathway information is obtained from public databases such as Gene Ontology and KEGG, and from the literature. Proprietary databases can be employed if the license requirements are satisfied.
